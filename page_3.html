Thriving at Umich

Discover how to thrive at the University of Michigan with these resources and support services.

University Health & Counseling (UHC) supports the health and well-being of the campus community through a public health-informed approach that emphasizes prevention, education, and treatment. We are inspired to help students realize their potential, cope with the stresses of life, work productively, and connect meaningfully with others.

UHC is made up of University Health Service (UHS), which offers medical services, and Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS), which offers counseling services.

University Health Service (UHS)

University Health Service (UHS) is your campus health and wellness resource, offering comprehensive clinical services plus support for your well-being. And most services are free for currently enrolled University of Michigan students (Ann Arbor campus).

  • Appointments are required. Schedule through your patient portal or by calling 734-764-8320.
  • Nurse advice by phone is available day or night.
  • Video visits are available for select services.

Medical Services

UHS offers a wide range of medical services, including primary care, specialty clinics, and pharmacy services. Whether you need a routine check-up, treatment for an illness or injury, or help managing a chronic condition, UHS has you covered.

Wellness Services

UHS offers a variety of wellness services to help you stay healthy and happy, from nutrition counseling and stress management to sexual health education and tobacco cessation. Whether you're looking to improve your physical health, mental well-being, or overall quality of life, UHS has the resources you need to thrive.

Health Promotion and Community Engagement

UHS offers a range of health promotion programs and services to help you make healthy choices and build a supportive community. Whether you're looking to learn more about nutrition, fitness, or mental health, UHS has the resources you need to stay well and thrive.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Make an Appointment

Urgent Support

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) offers a range of mental health services to support your emotional well-being and academic success. Whether you're struggling with stress, anxiety, depression, or other mental health concerns, CAPS has the resources you need to cope, heal, and thrive.

Individual Counseling

CAPS offers individual counseling services to help you explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors in a safe and confidential setting. Whether you're dealing with relationship issues, academic stress, or personal challenges, CAPS counselors are here to listen, support, and empower you to make positive changes in your life.

Group Counseling

CAPS offers a variety of group counseling services to help you connect with others, share your experiences, and learn new coping skills. Whether you're looking to improve your social skills, manage your anxiety, or build your self-esteem, CAPS groups provide a supportive and inclusive space for personal growth and healing.

Workshops and Events

CAPS offers a range of workshops and events to help you learn new skills, connect with others, and explore your interests. Whether you're looking to improve your communication skills, manage your time more effectively, or practice mindfulness, CAPS workshops and events provide valuable resources for personal growth and well-being.

Uwill Teletherapy Services

Uwill Teletherapy Services offers online counseling services to help you access mental health support from the comfort of your own home. Whether you're unable to come to campus, prefer the convenience of online therapy, or need immediate support, Uwill Teletherapy Services provides confidential and secure counseling services to help you cope, heal, and thrive.
